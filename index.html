01The Studio

A design department you never had to hire.

Est. 1997 · Conestoga, Pennsylvania

The CAD Connection has been drawing for manufacturers since 1997. SolidWorks has been our primary design tool since its inception, backed by AutoCAD for 2D layout and architectural work. Nearly three decades on the same tools means models that are clean, constrained, and easy for your team to pick up.

We work as an extension of your shop: quick quotes, fixed scopes, and drawings your fabricators can build from without calling us back with questions. Small enough to answer the phone. Experienced enough to catch the problem before it reaches the floor.

On time. Within budget. Built to print. That has been the commitment since day one.
